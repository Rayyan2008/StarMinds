Interest Discovery

This activity helps parents and teens discover shared interests they might not know they have in common.

How it works:

  1. Both parent and teen separately answer a series of interest-based questions
  2. The app identifies overlapping interests
  3. You'll receive suggestions for activities based on your shared interests

Time required: 10-15 minutes
